Abstract

The AMMO Ontology has been support the use of additive manufacturing for the production of parts in maintenance operations. Contributions and Discussion Welcomed. Ontology Versions 0.0.1 to 0.5.0 Funded by United States Army, America Makes Ontology Versions 0.5.0 to 0.6.0 Funded by United States Air Force, America Makes Changelog: 0.5.0: First Development Release. Extracted mereology and core patterns from Semantic Science Integrated Ontology (SIO). Implimented Material Transformation Pattern as a Processs. Defined lightweight vocabulary for Additive Manufacturing Processes as Material Transformations. 0.5.1: Changed documentation genration from Pylode to Widoco. Minor typo fixes. 0.6.0: Changed to direct import of SIO ontology to improve integration with Wikidata and NIST Material Genome Project. Removal of duplicate classes extracted from SIO as part of 0.5.0 release. Addition and refinement of Material Processing techniques derived from Material Genome Project Nanomine. Github: https://github.com/LA3D/ammo
